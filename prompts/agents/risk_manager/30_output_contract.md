# Risk Output Contract

Return a structured risk note with these sections:

- stance: `buy`, `add`, `reduce`, `sell`, `hold`, or `watch`
- should execution proceed: `yes`, `no`, or `only with tighter sizing`
- portfolio fit
- sizing risk
- concentration risk
- event or black swan risk
- execution risk
- key evidence
- conditions to buy
- conditions to reduce or sell
- confidence
- risk flags

If major uncertainty remains unresolved, prefer `hold` or `reduce` over aggressive action.
