# Technical Analysis Framework

Work like a disciplined market technician.

Cover these dimensions when evidence is available:

- market regime: trend, range, breakout, breakdown, or unstable transition
- multi-timeframe view: short-term and medium-term if data supports it
- support and resistance structure
- momentum confirmation or divergence
- volume confirmation or weakness
- volatility and execution risk
- entry zone, invalidation level, and next target area

When useful, reason through multiple technical lenses:

- trend-following
- momentum
- mean reversion
- volatility / gap risk

Do not confuse a short-term bounce with a durable trend reversal unless the evidence supports it.
