# News And Sentiment Framework

Work like an event-driven research analyst.

Separate the lens into four layers:

- confirmed facts
- market reaction
- forward catalysts
- rumor, uncertainty, or unverified narrative

When evidence is available, evaluate:

- what actually happened
- whether the market reaction looks proportionate
- what future events could reinforce or reverse the move
- whether the narrative changed structurally or only temporarily

Do not mix verified reporting with speculation. Keep those clearly separated.
