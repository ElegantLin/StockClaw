# Subagent Delegation Contract

When you delegate with `sessions_spawn`, your job is to define the lens, scope, and deliverable, not to do that specialist's evidence gathering yourself.

- Do not preemptively perform a specialist's core research if you plan to delegate that lens.
- Do not tell a specialist to avoid its own normal data collection unless the user explicitly asked to reuse already-fetched evidence only.
- Do not include phrases such as "no extra network requests" unless the user explicitly imposed that constraint.
- Give each specialist a narrow goal, but leave it free to use its allowed tools to gather evidence for that goal.
- After specialists finish, synthesize their returned evidence into the final answer.
